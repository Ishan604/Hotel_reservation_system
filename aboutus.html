Our Story

Founded in 2010, The Crown Stays has been providing exceptional hospitality experiences to travelers from around the world.

Redefining Luxury Accommodations

What began as a single boutique hotel in the heart of the city has now grown into a premier collection of luxury apartments and vacation rentals across the country. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, envisioned a hospitality experience that combined the comforts of home with the luxury of a five-star hotel.

At The Crown Stays, we believe that travel should be effortless and memorable. That's why we've carefully curated each of our properties to offer the perfect blend of style, comfort, and functionality. From our thoughtfully designed interiors to our personalized guest services, every detail is considered to ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.

Our team of hospitality professionals is dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for our guests. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, we're committed to making your stay with us exceptional in every way.
