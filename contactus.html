Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and policies.

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Our standard check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Do you offer airport transportation services?

Yes, we provide luxury airport transfers for our guests. Please arrange this service at least 48 hours prior to your arrival by contacting our concierge team.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 72 hours before arrival will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours will incur a one-night charge. Special rates and packages may have different cancellation policies.

Are pets allowed in your apartments?

We welcome pets in select apartments with prior approval. A non-refundable pet fee of $150 applies, and certain restrictions may apply based on size and breed.

Do you have facilities for business meetings?

Yes, we offer fully equipped meeting rooms with audiovisual capabilities, high-speed internet, and catering services. Our business center is available 24/7 for all guests.
